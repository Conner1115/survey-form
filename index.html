A few years later, we moved to Texas and my mom found out about freecodecamp. it was pretty hard to stay at freecodecamp and throw away khan academy. Within two years, my account got banned about three times, I gave up at it and stuck to freecodecamp and codepen of course. Now I'm thirteen and I am trying to learn as much as possible at FCC. I thank all of the kind, smart, and awesome community at the Freecodecamp Forum for their cool tips and help.
